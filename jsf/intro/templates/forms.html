Masters of Computer Application (M.C.A)

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a three-year (six semesters) professional Master's Degree in computer science awarded in India. The course was designed to meet the growing demand for qualified professionals in the field of Information Technology.

Master of Science in Information Technology(M.Sc IT)

A Master of Science in Information Technology is a type of postgraduate academic master's degree usually offered in a University's College of Business and in the recent years in integrated Information Science & Technology colleges

Masters of Science in Software Engineering(M.Sc SE)

The MSc in Software Engineering teaches the principles of modern software engineering, together with the tools, methods and techniques that support their application. It offers working professionals the opportunity to learn more about the technological advances that are changing and emerging.
